# Meals & Nutrition — Sunrise Early Learning

## Meals Provided

Sunrise provides **breakfast, lunch, and two snacks** daily, included in tuition.

| Meal | Time |
|------|------|
| Breakfast | 7:30 – 8:15 AM |
| Morning snack | 10:00 AM |
| Lunch | 11:45 AM – 12:30 PM |
| Afternoon snack | 3:00 PM |

Our menus are planned by a registered dietitian and rotate on a 4-week cycle. Weekly menus are posted in the Brightwheel app every Friday for the following week.

## Allergies and Dietary Restrictions

- All allergies must be documented on the enrollment form and updated annually.
- We are a **peanut-free facility**. Tree nut products are served only in designated rooms with staff supervision.
- We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and religious dietary needs. Please notify us during enrollment.
- Children with severe allergies (anaphylaxis risk) must have an **EpiPen on file** with a signed action plan from their pediatrician.

## Bringing Food from Home

Parents may send food from home if preferred. Please label all containers with the child's name and date. **No peanut or peanut-butter products** may be brought into the center.

Homemade food shared with other children (e.g., birthday treats) must include a full ingredient list.
