# Sick Child Policy — Sunrise Early Learning

The health and safety of every child in our care is our top priority.

## When to Keep Your Child Home

Your child **must stay home** if they have any of the following symptoms:

- **Fever** of **100.4°F (38°C) or higher**
- Vomiting or diarrhea within the past 24 hours
- Undiagnosed rash
- Eye discharge or pink eye (conjunctivitis)
- Head lice (until treated)
- Any contagious illness (strep throat, hand-foot-mouth, RSV, flu, COVID-19)

## Return Criteria

Children may return to Sunrise when they have been **symptom-free for at least 24 hours without the aid of fever-reducing medication** (such as Tylenol or Motrin).

For diagnosed contagious illnesses, a **doctor's note** clearing the child to return is required.

## If Your Child Becomes Sick at the Center

If a child develops symptoms while at Sunrise:

1. Staff will isolate the child in our designated wellness room.
2. A parent or emergency contact will be called **immediately**.
3. The child must be picked up **within one hour** of notification.
4. Staff will notify the child's classroom parents if an exposure to a contagious illness is confirmed.

## Medication Administration

We can administer prescribed medication with a signed authorization form from the parent. Over-the-counter medication requires both parent authorization and a doctor's note.
