# Scheduling a Tour — Sunrise Early Learning

We'd love to show you around! Tours are the best way to see our classrooms, meet our teachers, and learn about our curriculum.

## How to Schedule

- **Online:** Visit our website and fill out the tour request form, or simply ask here in chat and we'll have Director Maria reach out to you.
- **Phone:** Call us at (503) 555-0147 during business hours (Mon–Fri, 6:30 AM – 6:00 PM).
- **Email:** Send a message to maria@sunrise-daycare.example.

## Tour Details

- Tours are available **Monday through Friday, 9:30 AM – 11:00 AM and 3:30 PM – 5:00 PM**.
- Each tour lasts approximately **30 minutes**.
- You're welcome to bring your child along.
- We ask that only **2 adults per family** attend to keep group sizes manageable.

## What to Expect

During your tour, you'll:

1. See all of our classrooms (infant, toddler, and preschool)
2. Meet our lead teachers
3. Learn about our curriculum (play-based, emergent approach)
4. Review tuition, enrollment, and availability
5. Ask any questions you have

## Enrollment After Tour

If you'd like to enroll after your tour, we'll provide enrollment paperwork on the spot. A **$150 registration fee** and completed health/immunization records are required to secure your child's spot. Waitlist placement is available if your preferred age group is full.
