# Tuition — Sunrise Early Learning

## Monthly Tuition Rates (Full-Time, 5 days/week)

| Age Group   | Monthly Rate |
|-------------|-------------|
| Infant (6 weeks – 12 months) | $1,800/month |
| Toddler (12 months – 3 years) | $1,550/month |
| Preschool (3 – 5 years) | $1,300/month |

## Part-Time Options

- **3-day program (Mon/Wed/Fri):** 70% of full-time rate
- **2-day program (Tue/Thu):** 50% of full-time rate

## Payment

- Tuition is due on the **1st of each month**.
- We accept check, ACH bank transfer, and credit card (2.5% processing fee for credit cards).
- A **one-time registration fee of $150** is required upon enrollment.

## Sibling Discount

A **10% discount** on the lower tuition applies for families with two or more children enrolled simultaneously.

## Financial Assistance

We participate in Oregon's Employment Related Day Care (ERDC) subsidy program. Contact Director Maria for details.
