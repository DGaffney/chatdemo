# Holiday Closures — Sunrise Early Learning

Sunrise Early Learning is **closed** on the following holidays:

- **New Year's Day** — January 1
- **Martin Luther King Jr. Day** — Third Monday in January
- **Presidents' Day** — Third Monday in February
- **Memorial Day** — Last Monday in May
- **Independence Day** — July 4
- **Labor Day** — First Monday in September
- **Veterans Day** — November 11
- **Thanksgiving Day** — Fourth Thursday in November
- **Day After Thanksgiving** — Fourth Friday in November
- **Christmas Eve** — December 24
- **Christmas Day** — December 25

If a holiday falls on a weekend, we observe it on the nearest weekday (Friday for Saturday holidays, Monday for Sunday holidays).

Tuition is **not prorated** for holiday closures, as these are factored into the annual rate.

## Severe Weather Closures

In the event of severe weather (ice storms, heavy snow), we follow the **Portland Public Schools** closure schedule. If PPS closes, Sunrise closes. Notifications are sent via the Brightwheel app by 6:00 AM.
