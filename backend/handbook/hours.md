# Operating Hours — Sunrise Early Learning

Sunrise Early Learning is open **Monday through Friday, 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM**.

We are located at **4521 SE Hawthorne Blvd, Portland, OR 97215**.

## Drop-off and Pick-up

- **Drop-off window:** 6:30 AM – 9:00 AM. Children arriving after 9:00 AM should notify the front desk in advance so we can plan activities accordingly.
- **Pick-up deadline:** 6:00 PM sharp. A late fee of $1 per minute applies after 6:05 PM.
- **Early pick-up:** Parents are welcome to pick up at any time. Please sign out at the front desk.

## Weekend and Evening Care

We do not offer weekend or evening care at this time. If you need extended hours, please speak with Director Maria about referral partners.
