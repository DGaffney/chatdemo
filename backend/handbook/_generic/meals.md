# General Meals & Nutrition Information

Most licensed daycare centers provide meals and snacks as part of their program:

- Breakfast, lunch, and at least one snack are commonly included in tuition.
- Centers are required to accommodate documented food allergies.
- Many centers are nut-free or have nut-free classrooms.
- Parents can typically send food from home if preferred; labeling with the child's name is required.
- Weekly menus are usually shared with parents in advance.

For your center's specific meal program and allergy policies, please ask your director.
