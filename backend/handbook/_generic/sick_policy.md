# General Sick Child Policy

Most licensed daycare centers follow these common guidelines:

- Children with a fever of 100.4°F or higher should stay home.
- Children must be symptom-free (no fever, vomiting, or diarrhea) for at least 24 hours before returning.
- Contagious illnesses typically require a doctor's clearance note.
- Centers will call parents to pick up a sick child, usually within one hour of symptom onset.

For your center's specific policy, please ask your director.
